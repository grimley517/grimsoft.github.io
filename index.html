GrimSoft Web

A consultancy based in Hobart, Tasmania specialising in academic and educational web resources, as well as SMEs and non-profit organbisations.

With over 15 years experience in the education and technology industries Grimsoft is here for your web consultancy needs.

What makes education special?

Education is an important area which can be served by the modern internet, but many academics and students do not have the expertise to make full use of the web. Grimsoft is a specialist web consultancy which aims to deliver you the information support you need, within the tight scope of educational budgets.

Do you need a central document management system for your club, society, or other organisation. Do you wish to utilise the power of social media to extend the range of your enquiries, or to make your research more widely known, but don't want to broadcast your holiday snaps from facebook?
